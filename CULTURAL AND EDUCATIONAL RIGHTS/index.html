CULTURAL AND EDUCATIONAL RIGHTS



  • Article 29 provides that every section of citizens residing in any part of the country has the right to protect and conserve its own distinct language, script or culture (it provides the right to a group/section/community of people).
  • Article 30 grants minorities the right to establish an administer educational institutions and ensures non-discriminatory treatment by the State. The 44th Amendment Act, 1978, reinforced these protections.
