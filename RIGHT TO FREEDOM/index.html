RIGHT TO FREEDOM


  • Article 19 guarantees to all citizens the 6 rights of freedom including: (freedom of speech on expression, right to assemble peacefully and without arms, right to form associations or unions or co-operative societies, right to move freely throughout the territory of India, right to reside and settle in any part of the territory of India, right to practice any profession).
  • Article 20 grants protection against arbitrary and excessive punishment to an accused person, whether citizen or foreigner a legal person like a company or a corporation.
  • Article 21 declares that no person shall be deprived of his life or personal liberty except according to procedure established by law. This right is available for both citizens and non citizens.
  • Article 21(A) declares that the street shell provide free and compulsory education to all children of the age of 6 to 14 years.
  • Article 22 grants protection to persons were arrested or detained.
